Our Mission

What drives us forward

To provide quality education in computer science and engineering through innovative teaching, research, and collaboration with industry, preparing students to become competent professionals capable of addressing technological challenges and contributing to society. We strive to create an academic environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and ethical practices in computing.

Key Objectives

Deliver comprehensive and up-to-date curriculum

Promote research and innovation in computing fields

Establish strong industry-academia collaboration

Develop ethical and socially responsible professionals

Our Vision

Where we aim to be

To be a center of excellence in computer science and engineering education, recognized for producing innovative professionals, generating new knowledge through research, and serving as a resource for technological advancement in the region and beyond. We envision a department that leads in cutting-edge research while addressing local and global challenges through technology.
