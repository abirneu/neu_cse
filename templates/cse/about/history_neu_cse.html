History of CSE Department

Netrokona University

1

2008

Establishment

The Computer Science and Engineering Department was established as one of the founding departments of Netrokona University, with a vision to provide quality education in computing and technology.

1
2
2

2010

First Batch Admission

The department welcomed its first batch of 60 students into the undergraduate program, marking the beginning of a new era in technical education in the region.

3

2013

Lab Facilities Expansion

The department established two new computer labs with modern equipment, including high-performance computers and networking facilities to support practical learning.

3
4
4

2015

First Graduating Class

The department celebrated its first graduating class, with many students securing positions in renowned tech companies and pursuing higher studies at home and abroad.

5

2018

Research Initiatives

The department established its research wing, focusing on areas like artificial intelligence, data science, and network security, with several faculty members receiving research grants.

5
6
6

2020

Industry Collaboration

The department signed MOUs with several IT companies for internships, industrial training, and collaborative projects, enhancing the industry-academia relationship.

7

2023

Current Status

Today, the CSE Department at Netrokona University has grown to include 15 faculty members, over 400 students, and state-of-the-art laboratories, continuing its mission to produce skilled computer engineers.

7

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computer science and engineering through innovative teaching, research, and collaboration with industry, preparing students to become competent professionals capable of addressing technological challenges and contributing to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer science and engineering education, recognized for producing innovative professionals, generating new knowledge through research, and serving as a resource for technological advancement in the region and beyond.
