About Us

Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Netrokona University

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Netrokona University, established in 2010, has quickly emerged as a leading center for technology education in northern Bangladesh. As a modern department with forward-looking vision, CSE at Netrokona University is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation.

Our department attracts talented students from across the region and offers comprehensive Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science and Engineering. Our curriculum is regularly updated to meet international standards and industry demands, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
